An online generator to derive your Akan name

The Akan (/əˈkæn/) are a meta-ethnicity living in the southern regions of present-day Ghana, formerly known as the Gold Coast. Akans who migrated from Ghana also make up a plurality of the ethnic population in the Ivory Coast. The Akan language (also known as Twi/Fante) is a group of dialects within the Central Tano branch of the Potou–Tano subfamily of the Niger–Congo family.
